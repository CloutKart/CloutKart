# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

D2C brand founders, performance marketers, and growth-focused marketing managers who need high-converting ad creatives fast. They are results-driven, budget-conscious, and skeptical of agencies that over-promise. They've been burned by slow creative production and generic ads that don't convert. They know their numbers (ROAS, CTR, CAC) and make decisions based on them.

## Product Purpose

CloutKart is a creative operations platform for D2C brands: submit a brief, Pixie (the AI creative director) generates hook, color story, and visual direction, then production-ready ad creatives are delivered in 48 hours. The platform solves the D2C creative bottleneck — slow iteration cycles, inconsistent messaging, and disconnected creative teams. Success looks like a brand going from "guessing what converts" to running a repeatable, scalable creative engine.

## Brand Personality

Aggressive · Precise · Craft

Like a rally car driven by an art director. High-performance, conversion-obsessed, but with genuine craft underneath. Not a generic SaaS. Not a polite agency. Direct-response energy with a quality finish.

Emotional goal for visitors: *confidence that this will actually work*, followed by urgency to start.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS purple — soft gradients, clean Notion-style white space, minimal, polite. The default AI landing page look (which the current site partially resembles). Avoid anything that feels like a wireframe with a purple tint.

Also avoid: corporate agency (soulless blue/white), over-designed experimental portfolio (art for art's sake — this is a performance tool), Web3/crypto maximalism.

## Design Principles

1. **Practise what you preach.** The site IS an ad. It must hook in seconds, communicate the value clearly, and convert. If it doesn't do what CloutKart promises for its clients, the brand falls apart.
2. **Performance signals are the hero.** Metrics, numbers, speed, and results are not supporting copy — they are the visual design elements that build trust. Show the data.
3. **No filler.** Every section, every element earns its place. Like a good ad, nothing decorates for decoration's sake.
4. **Craft is visible.** The quality of the site signals the quality of the creative output. Precision in typography, motion, and spacing is evidence of craft, not just aesthetics.
5. **Message first.** The platform's core philosophy applies to its own design: identify the one thing visitors need to understand at each point, and make that one thing unmissable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all animations — crossfade or instant transition as fallback. Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for body text, ≥ 3:1 for large text. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable and ≥ 44×44px touch target.
